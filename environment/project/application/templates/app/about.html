About Us


The Elephant House, or Ceylon Cold Stores PLC (CCS), is a Sri Lankan giant in the food and beverage industry. They've been around since 1866, starting with ice production and evolving into a household name synonymous with delicious treats. From their market-leading Elephant Ginger Ale to a wide range of ice creams and even some processed meats, they offer something for everyone. Their focus is on providing tasty and nutritious options at reasonable prices, making them a staple in Sri Lankan homes. With a commitment to quality and a rich heritage, Elephant House continues to delight taste buds across the country.


Mission


Vision and practices reveal a clear purpose. They prioritize bringing pleasure and nourishment to people's lives through exciting and high-quality food and beverages. This translates to offering a diverse range of delicious products, including healthier options that cater to those seeking a more balanced diet. Affordability and variety are key, ensuring their tasty treats are accessible to a broad spectrum of Sri Lankan consumers. In essence, Elephant House aspires to be a constant companion, providing delightful and nutritious options for every step of life's journey.
